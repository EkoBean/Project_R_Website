Build, Govern, Thrive — Welcome to Ratopia!

There was long time ago

After centuries of hiding from the surface world, rats have forged a new path below ground. As the founder of a new settlement, you decide how history remembers your reign. Will your colony be a beacon of hope — or a totalitarian empire?

Game System

Extend the Territory

Uncover hidden resources, ancient ruins, and secrets that expand your kingdom’s reach. Exploration is the first step toward greatness.

Shape Your City

Plan, place, and grow your settlement. Every structure serves your rats—comfort, production, or prestige.

Defend and Conquer

Raise armies, repel threats, and prove your might in tactical combat against wild beasts and rival clans.

Manage and Trade

Balance production, sustain growth, and control the flow of goods. A thriving economy powers your kingdom.
